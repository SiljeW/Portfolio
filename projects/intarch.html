    Project Overview

    Intarch is a blog platform where the mission is to share accurate insights, ideas and thoughts within related fields.

    The target audience includes Interior Architecture professionals who are eager to learn and share with others. The website creates a space for important topics in how interior architeture affects the society.

    This website was a project exam after the first year of Front-End Development studies, and had the criterias to demonstrate knowledge within Figma design and prototype, semantic HTML, CSS and Javascript.

    Key Features

    • Responsive design for all device sizes
    • Open for all to read all blogs
    • Registration is limited to a certain email-address
    • Streamlined blog editor and publisher process
